Avalanche Pizza

When looking for a great slice of pizza, Avalanche Pizza will certaintly deliver. This by far is one of my favorite pizza shops within the whole Athens area. Pizza is reasonably priced and all the ingredients are locally sourced. And not to mention their made-from sratch dough. Grab yourself a slice while you are here in Athens!
